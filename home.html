Welcome to Coffee, the last place you'll ever buy coffee!

Our philosophy at Coffee is to provide the highest quality beans at any cost. For this reason, we travel the world in search of perfection, hand-picking literally every individual bean we bring back. We source from more than 3 countries: Ethiopia, Costa Rica, Colombia, and Guatemala.

We also carry a large assortment of brewing gear to meet your needs. Our selection includes a wide variety of brewing tools, as well as burr grinders, kettles, and accessories.
